About Me & My Platform

Hello! I'm Lrv Sai Kausthubh, the developer behind the Cafeteria Management System. I love creating technology solutions that solve real-world problems.


I wanted to create a system that makes food ordering simple and efficient for both customers and cafeteria staff.


Why I Built This?

I noticed the long queues and waiting times at our cafeteria, and realized that a digital solution could greatly improve the experience for everyone. This platform was created to:


  • Reduce waiting times for students and staff
  • Simple online ordering process
  • Provide better inventory management for the cafeteria
  • Create a platform for easy menu updates and special offers

Key Features

Simple Shopping Cart

Intuitive shopping experience with real-time updates

Multiple Payment Options

Secure payment processing with various payment methods

Order History & Tracking

Complete visibility into order status and history

Analytics Dashboard

Track performance metrics and export detailed reports to Improve operations

Live Quantity Updates

Get Real Time information on how many items are left in stock

Gmail Parsing

Scans your gmail for order id and if it matches, It will automitacally update the order status
